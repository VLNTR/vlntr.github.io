### How we work

We bring together not-for-profits and small teams of VLNTRs to work on _Missions_. A Mission is objectively defined, and time limited to 30 days. A team is formed with a VLNTR from each of our Research (#RE), Product (#PR), Development (#DE) and Governance (#GO) groups.
